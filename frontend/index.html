Check your phone.

Plug your phone into this computer with a USB cable and run a read-only security scan: spyware indicators, stalkerware, risky settings. It shows you, in plain language, what is solid and what to fix first. Nothing is sent anywhere unless you choose to.

Looking for your phone…
On your Android phone:
  1. Settings · About phone · tap 'Build number' 7 times to enable Developer options.
  2. Settings · System · Developer options · enable 'USB debugging'.
  3. Connect with a USB DATA cable, then tap 'Allow' on the phone.

When you finish: Developer options · 'Revoke USB debugging authorizations', then turn USB debugging off. The report reminds you.

iPhone detected. Unlock it and tap 'Trust' if asked. For the full spyware scan, MobileCheck makes a temporary encrypted backup, analyzes it with Amnesty's MVT against known spyware indicators, and deletes it right after.

One-time iOS setup

To analyze iPhones, MobileCheck installs Amnesty's open-source tools (pymobiledevice3 and MVT) into a private environment on THIS computer, and downloads the public spyware indicators. These are the only two times the app uses the network, both download-only. Nothing about you or your phone is sent.

  • pypi.org: pymobiledevice3 + MVT (hash-pinned)
  • github.com/mvt-project: spyware indicators (IoCs)
